      Sources. Facilities and any violation counts are drawn from the U.S. EPA Facility Registry Service (FRS) and Enforcement & Compliance History Online (ECHO); planning items are drawn from county, city and Utah Public Meeting Notice records. Each item links to its official public record. A violation figure is a factual count taken from the public EPA ECHO record — not a verdict on any operator. Nothing here is legal advice or an allegation of wrongdoing. HomeSignal renders public records; it does not generate claims about any facility.
