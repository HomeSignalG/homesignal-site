What you get in every community.

Two ways in — the same pairing you'll find on your dashboard for each place you follow.

See it

Development

See what's happening around your home.

  • Type in your address or ZIP and instantly see everything being built near you on one easy-to-read map
  • Get a heads-up the moment projects open for public comment — and how many days you have left to weigh in
  • Know your area at a glance: industrial sites near you, what's newly proposed, what's open for comment, and the total on record
  • View your neighborhood as a flat map, aerial, or satellite — and zoom in or out to focus on what's closest to home
  • See everything sorted into three plain buckets: operating now, approved and coming, and still proposed
  • Know exactly when each comment window closes, so you never miss your chance to speak up
  • Click any facility to see who it is, its status, and its violations — then jump to the official EPA or government record in one click to verify

Follow it

Alerts

Only hear about what matters to you.

  • Choose exactly what lands in your inbox instead of getting one firehose of everything
  • Turn whole categories on or off — government notices, upcoming meetings, local news
  • Fine-tune the topics you follow: planning & zoning, property taxes, public safety, data centers, water quality, and more
  • Follow the bigger picture too — how peer communities handle the same issues, and new tech & infrastructure proposals
  • Watch a live feed of what's new, split into what's happening right in your ZIP vs. regional and national context
  • See upcoming meetings with dates, spot the new ones at a glance, and sign up to attend or follow a specific one
  • Save your community once and come back anytime, tuned to exactly what you want to know
