How it works

From public record to your inbox.

Three steps to stay ahead of decisions that affect your home.

1

Find your community

Search by county, city, ZIP code, or project name to open your community page.

2

Choose the alerts that matter

Pick the meeting types and topics you care about — council meetings, data center development, water quality, and more.

3

Get notified early

We watch permit filings, government notices, and local news, and send you a heads-up while there is still time to act.

Where the information comes from

HomeSignal pulls from official sources such as government public notice websites and local news, then organizes them into a single, readable feed for your community.
